writing ◌

untitled

your mind
a landscape
green and innate
long before the
waters
wars submerging
and submarines
drowning
ecstasy flutters
and dies with
kaleidoscopic
synesthesia
a sharpness of light
dims
every last
dream
in transit
once a
dreamer
now you
just
dream
I seek you out
but you cannot see me
my feckless gestures
are ghosts to you
you were once my
postcard home
your reply an
anchor to life
but now the letters
are smudged
and submerged
lost at sea

but in our other world
we can laugh again
and play chess by
the rolling tides of antiquity
our lyrical talk
a lullaby
carried by
the distant winds
to another universe

untitled

telepathic enlightenment
congealing and conjugating
all the letters
of our syntax
of man’s semantics
of the mind
and the infinite
unfolding of the universe
of the one consciousness
ascending all the hype
transcending intergalactic imagination
immunity
incredulity
as we turn
higher higher
into false impiety
higher higher
into this blinded society
unthinkin’ and unblinkin’
we trudge through this life
as cattle we live to work
throats cut backs bent
bones of malice
lives drag deep
we’re callous
munchin’ on this burnt earth
for nothin’

we’re couped up:
this ain’t change
this is a devolution
this ain’t insurgency
this is all an illusion
but could one carefree thought
rise up liberate us all
create that catatonic emergency
that catalyst
that convergency
crashing down
with metabolic force
everything we’ve always believed in
killing us
cutting us free
that strife
that entanglement
until we see
but it’s
too ingrained
everything remains
the same
we’re all bound:
indoctrination
for the rest of the game
